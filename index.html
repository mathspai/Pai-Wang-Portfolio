Portfolio Statement

My projects this semester explore how natural spaces are translated through digital media. I photographed ponds, flowers, paths, branches, and shifting light, then used those observations as raw material for motion, documentary, graphic design, and interactive storytelling. Across the portfolio, the garden becomes both a real place and a data set: something remembered, compressed, edited, and re-entered through the screen.
